Personal Profile

Myer-Briggs Results

My Myer-Briggs results show that my personality type is INTP-T, also referred to as the Logician. This states that I am more introverted than extroverted, more intuitive than observant, more rational than emotional, far more prospecting than judging and much more turbulent than assertive. This result is extremely accurate as I am often fairly inquisitive and find myself not only questioning everything but often thinking too much and unable to shut off my brain.

Learning Style Assessment Results

The results of my learning style assessment state that my learning style is visual. This implies that in order for me to learn and remember well, a visual aid of some kind usually assists greatly. I find this fairly accurate as I often tend to forget what someone has said to me but I will remember things that I have seen in much greater detail.

Career Personality Test Results

The final test I chose to take was a Career Personality Test. The results of this show that my work style is largely humanitarian and innovator. This means that my work has to have a positive outcome on people, and be beneficial and insightful. It also highlights my enjoyment of complex issues and analyitical problem solving.

When working in a group, in order to optimally complete tasks, an organised document outlining every goal that is needed to be completed would provide beneficial support to myself in understanding what I must do next to complete the task. Along with this, discussion of ideas in depth would allow me to have a greater understanding of what each team members goal and idea was, as well as what is required for them to accomplish these tasks as I like to have the answers to questions such as that.
